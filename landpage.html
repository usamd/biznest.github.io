About BizNest

BizNest is a focused business directory designed to support entrepreneurs and customers across Sri Lanka. Our mission is to make it simple for people to find reputable local services and for businesses to reach the right audience.

  • Local & verified listings
  • Simple search and filtering
  • Optional promoted placement for growth

Mission

To support everyone in their initial steps — people willing to start or grow a business by giving them discoverability and essential tools.

Vision

A thriving local economy where every small business can be found and chosen by customers easily.
